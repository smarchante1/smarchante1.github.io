About Steph

"My name is Stephanie. I'm self-taught bassist by trade. Here's my story."

Slapping and popping a five string bass, playing octave chords and holding down the low end are my main areas of expertise. Once upon a time I was certain that would be my primary career focus. The world of jazz and funk is so massive, what could I possibly be doing aside from exploring it all?

That all changed when I received the incredible opportunity to tour nationally and record bass lines as a session player for some amazing musicians. Suddenly, music technology became a part of my vernacular. And even further, software became something I was surrounded with daily. I became enamored with the idea of understanding the functionality of music programs like Logic Pro and Pro Tools. Just playing bass was no longer enough. I grew an insatiable appetite for understanding just what was going on under the hood of these programs that made my bass sound so darn good!

I decided through many weeks of existential pondering and late night jam sessions that all roads led to Software Development. This path, I thought, would bridge that gap between my being a musician and really understanding how all of the software programs I use really work.
